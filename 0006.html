Author Notes

Don't ever make agreements with players because they will use it against you.

Charter of Rights Of The Gaming Table, under section 2,a,.ii.b.

If there is an exceptionally long NPC scene, more commonly known as a cutscene, the players may, if unanimous, force the DM to skip, or, if the DM deems the 'cutscene' important, skim over it and only say important information.

Transcript

Robert: This is getting boring. Let's move on.

DM: Come on! Remember what I explained about backstory, Robert?

Robert: Under the Charter of Rights Of The Gaming Table, under section 2,a,.ii.b, The players, if unanimous, can force the DM to skip a NPC. Peter, do you Agree with me?

Peter: Yeah! This is dumb!

Robert: You've been outvoted, DM. Now get a move on.

DM: Fine! Honestly, so impatient.
